Mobile platforms and technologies present unique challenges and opportunities, overcoming these challenges and leveraging the opportunities are key to success. The Customer Experience is taking center stage. Brands and agencies alike are quickly realigning their mobile strategies to capture the opportunity technologies have presented and leapfrog the competition.

In New York we'll discuss...

Brands and Customer Engagement

The mobile phone has intimate user knowledge and offers to brands the chance to resonate emotionally with customers. Customers know this, in part because thousands of brand messages vie for their attention daily, and it’s almost a task to stand guard. Hear from brands that have differentiated through mobile to craft identity, and learn about mobile platform behaviors to bolster your segmentation process.

All About Timing? Pieces to the Contextualization Puzzle

Right message to the right person in the right place at the right time. No problem? The journey might start on desktop, but an evolving and aspirational message winding its way through a customer’s day has tremendous guiding influence. Move beyond a multichannel strategy by mastering an omnichannel approach where small improvements have disproportionate benefits.

Cross-Regional Investment Perspectives and the Customer’s Role

Investors from Sand Hill Road to Wall Street are making big bets and anointing billion dollar ideas with greater frequency than years past. In this session, investors square off on what makes a great startup, a great exit, and a great public company. To be sure, an unbeatable customer experience on mobile is a common thread.

Making Beacons Work for you

Beacons are mixing up marketers’ sense of place. Targeting moving customers with location technology promises to boost retail sales but also adds a layer of complexity to customer relationships. With dropping prices, beacon technology could become commonplace, and customers will want to know they’re being looked out for.

Winning Customers in Asia

Explore the opportunities and challenges of expanding in key regions in Asia, including China. Veteran speakers who have found success in Asia will present strategies for acquiring customers and building enthusiastic customer communities. The discussion will cover complexities of the Asian market and unique market opportunities for those that get it right.

Millennial Wallets

Whether time or behavior best marks the boundaries of a generation, it is clear the millennial’s have marked their place in the minds of marketers. How do you best serve an autonomous, responsive, tech savvy population? These 80 million U.S. customers that grew up computing will soon have the most spending power, and engaging them on mobile is a must. We’ll cover the new tech and techniques you need to stay a step ahead of this misrepresented, pragmatic bunch.
